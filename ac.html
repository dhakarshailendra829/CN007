Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the Chicken
In a large bowl, combine chicken pieces with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2 - Sauté the Aromatics
Heat oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and ginger, and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.

Step 3 - Add Spices and Cook
Stir in curry powder, cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Cook for 30 seconds, until fragrant.

Step 4 - Add Wet Ingredients and Simmer
Add diced tomatoes, coconut milk, tomato paste, and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 20 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.

Step 5 - Serve
Season with salt to taste. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot over cooked rice.
