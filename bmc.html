Ingredients:
  • 250 grams paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed

  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
    		•
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
    		•
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
    		•
  • 1 tomato, chopped
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    		•
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
    		•
  • Salt to taste
    		•
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (fresh coriander leaves)
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon water (optional, for adjusting consistency)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Fry the Paneer
Heat oil in a pan or skillet over medium heat. Add the cubed paneer and fry until golden brown on all sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.

Step 2 - Saut√© the Aromatics
In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed. Add chopped onions and cook until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.

Step 3 - Add Spices and Tomato
Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become mushy and release their juices.

Step 4 - Add Dry Spices and Cook
Stir in ground coriander, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Cook for another minute until the spices become fragrant.

Step 5 - Add Peas and Water (Optional)
Add green peas and cook for 2-3 minutes until peas are heated through. If the gravy feels too thick, add a tablespoon of water to adjust the consistency.

Step 6 - Final Touches
Add fried paneer cubes back to the pan.
